Dispute thresholds

Set dispute-rate bands that trigger monitoring, suspension, or termination actions for merchants.

Normal threshold

Merchants in this band have a dispute rate within acceptable limits. No extra action is required.

Minimum percentage: >0%
Maximum percentage: <=0.2%

Warning threshold

Merchants entering this band should be monitored. Consider outreach before dispute volume grows.

Minimum percentage: >0.2%
Maximum percentage: <=0.5%

Suspension threshold

Merchants in this band may be suspended from processing until dispute rates improve.

Minimum percentage: >0.5%
Maximum percentage: <=0.9%

Termination threshold

Merchants above this band are at risk of account termination due to sustained high dispute rates.

Minimum percentage: >0.9%
Maximum percentage: >0.9%
